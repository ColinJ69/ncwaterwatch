Impacts of Hurricane Helene on Western NC's water quality

10/4/24

Status of water in peoples homes

The millions who call the western part of the state home have been struck hard by the recent devastating Hurricane Helene.
As a result of this, hundreds of lives and millions in infrastructure and homes have been lost. As of today, many residents are stuck with insane levels of flooding and a lack of vital resources including clean water, food, and stable shelter. On the topic of water, residents have been advised to boil water before drinking it, and to test their wells before drinking from them. A large percentage of the residents have as such opted to drink from bottled water from local distrubution centers.
